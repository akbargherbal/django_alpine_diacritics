About تَشْكِيل

An interactive platform designed to simplify the process of adding diacritical marks to Arabic text.

What is Arabic Diacritization?

Arabic diacritization (تَشْكِيل) involves adding vowel marks and other phonetic guides to Arabic text. These marks are crucial for:

  • Proper pronunciation
  • Disambiguation of word meanings
  • Correct reading for learners
كتب → كَتَبَ (wrote) / كُتُب (books)
